A-site codon

The codon that is being translated whose ribosome density is being estimated by quantification of mapped reads.

A-site offset

The distance between the 5' (or 3') end and the A-site codon while maintaining the same reading frame.

Fragment size

The size of the read obtained in the Ribo-Seq Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) experiment that represents a copy of the mRNA fragment protected by the ribosome and cleaved by the RNase enzyme.

Frame

The reading frame in which the 5' end of the read is.

P-site codon

The codon immediately preceding the codon present in the A-site. This codon has been decoded in the ribosome and the corresponding amino acid will form a peptide bond with the amino acid encoded by the codon in the A-site.
